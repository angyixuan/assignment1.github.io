Punggol Waterway Park

A project by National Parks and HDB, Punggol Waterway Park covers an expansive 12.25 hectares and boasts of lush greenery. The multi-purpose park is divided into four different themes: Nature Cove, Recreation Zone, Heritage Zone and Green Gallery. Set away from bustling traffic, the park provides a serene and relaxing environment for all to enjoy.

Fitness enthusiasts will find it a joy to traverse the jogging and cycling tracks as they are surrounded by nature as well as the beautiful water frontages.

There are child-friendly areas such as the sand play area and a water playground as well.

Punggol Promenade

Part of the National Park Connectors, Punggol Promenade has many attractions, including water lily ponds and iconic bridges. Visitors can also embark on the Punggol Promenade Nature Walk here. The trail overlooks Coney Island, and has three rest shelters that resemble seashells. Fishing platforms are located along the trail as well.

Coney Island

The rustic Coney Island park is an ecologically sustainable park, with up to eighty species of birds found in its various habitats. Some plant species are even presumed to be nationally extinct in the wild!

Lorong Halus Wetland

The Lorong Halus Wetland collects and treats water, preventing it from flowing into the nearby reservoir. This acts as a safeguard for the reservoir's water quality. Visitors can embark on a self-guided learning trail to learn more about water sustainability. More information can be found here.
